Client

CoCreate HGK Basel FHNW

The CoCreate brochure contains all information about the CoCreate programme at HGK Basel FHNW. My contribution included both the typesetting and the development of the visual language.

The distorted scan images of tools offer a visual exploration of the moment when creation begins to take shape, reflecting the fluid interplay of individual skills that defines CoCreate.

The illustrative layer was added at the final stage in collaboration with Silvan Borer.
