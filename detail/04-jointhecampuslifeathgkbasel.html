“Join the Campus Life at HGK Basel ” is the 2025 / 26 annual campaign. Building on the design concept by Celine Pereira and Pati Grabowicz ( Studio Moono ), I developed animations for a range of applications.

The campaign evolved through a series of phases that corresponded to key events at HGK Basel FHNW.

Beyond animated posters and web applications, the animations were used at the 2025 graduation ceremony, in various presentation formats, and on public transport screens in Basel.
