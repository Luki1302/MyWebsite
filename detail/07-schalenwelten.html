The “ Schalenwelten ” exhibition space is a fictitious project in collaboration with the Natural History Museum Basel. It invites visitors to experience the diversity and beauty of shells and snails in a special way.

The exhibition concept is extended through additional communication tools, such as an EPUB and a poster. These translate the idea into visual and informative formats.

The physical display case model and the digital visualisations are part of an extended study, helping to make the interactive space more tangible for visitors.
